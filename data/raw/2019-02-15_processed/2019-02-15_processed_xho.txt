Amajita azibekele indawo emva kokudlulela kumdlalo ophambi kowamagqibela kankqoyi weNdebe yoMbutho weBhola eKhatywayo yase-Afrika yabangaPhantsi kwama-20 eminyaka (Confederation of African Football Under-20 Africa Cup of Nations) eqhubeka e-Niger ngoku.
