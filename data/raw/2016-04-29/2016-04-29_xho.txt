IKhabhinethi ikulinde ngomdla ukuqukunjelwa kweenkqubo zokugunyaziswa zalapha ekhaya, ngawo onke, ukuvumela ukungena kwangexesha ukwenzela ukufezekiswa ngokupheleleyo kwesiVumelwano saseParis ngowama-2020.1.8. Izigqibo zeKhabhinethi ezingundoqo2.1. IKhabhinethi isamkele isiCwangciso soMgaqo-nkqubo wokuSebenza kwezoShishino (i-IPAP) sowama-2016/17 – 2018/19, nesiyintsika engundoqo yesiCwangciso esimaNqaku aliThoba sikaMongameli kwaye senza igalelo ekuphunyezweni kwesiCwangciso seSizwe soPhuhliso.                              Oku kukuphindaphindwa kwaminyaka le kwesibhozo kwesicwangciso sikarhulumente semizi-mveliso esiyimpumelelo nesakhelwa kwiimpumelelo, amaqonga emigaqo-nkqubo neenkqubo zokuphindaphindwa kwangaphambili.I-IPAP yowama-2016/17 iinjongo zayo kukwenza impembelelo enkulu kumgaqo-nkqubo wezoshishino phantsi kweemeko ezinzima zezoqoqosho. Ekukho phakathi kwazo impembelelo eqhubekayo yokudodobala kwezoqoqosho kwihlabathi jikelele kuquka iimeko emaxongo kurhwebo lwezesinyithi kunye nezimbiwa kwakunye nembalela enkulu neminye imiqobo ebalulekileyo yalapha ekhaya.Imisebenzi eyimpumelelo yonyaka ophelileyo iquka ukusindiswa kwemisebenzi emalunga nama-67 000 nokudalwa kwemisebenzi emitsha endilisekileyo ethelekelelwa kuma-7 000 kwicandelo leMpahla nelezeziKhumba neZihlangu. Icandelwana elisebenza phantsi kokucolwa nokulungiswa kweemveliso zezolimo lelinye lamacandelo okuvelisa amakhulu apha ekhaya ngokwengqesho, abonelela ngemisebenzi ethelekelelwa kuma-216 090 ngowama-2015 xa kuthelekiswa nama-208 822 ngowama-2014. Lutyalo-mali olungaphezulu kwezigidi ezingama-600 zeerandi athe atsalwe ngowama-2015/16, elusisiseko sokomeleza uphuhliso lwamashishini amancinci olugxile kuluntu lwasemaphandleni. 2.2. Khabhinethi iyamkele iNkqubo-sikhokelo yokuGqibela yoPhuhliso lweeDolophu oluDibeneyo nesiCwangciso sokuPhumeza sowama-2016/19. Iziphumo ezipheleleyo zeNkqubo –sikhokelo yoPhuhliso lweDolophu oludibeneyo yinguqu ngokwabala omhlaba. Oku kuza notshintsho olutsha kwizixeko needolophu zaseMzantsi Afrika, nokuza kuqhubela uhlumo lwasezidolophini kwindlela eluqilima yohlumo oluzinzileyo, iqhagamshelanise ize iququzelele izixeko needolophu, neziyindawo ekhuselekileyo, ezinemithombo esebenzayo futhi neziyindawo elungileyo ukuba umntu angasebenza ahlale kuzo.                 Oku kusabela kumyalelo wesiCwangciso seSizwe soPhuhliso wokuqulunqa umgaqo-nkqubo wophuhliso lwedolophu oza kuqinisekisa ngokucwangcisa okufanelekileyo, utyalo-mali kwiziseko ezingundoqo nolawulo lokuxhasa uhlumo nokuvulela amandla ezoqoqosho lwezixeko needolophu.2.3. IKhabhinethi ikwamkele ukupapashwa kweDrafti yoMgaqo-nkqubo weSizwe weeNkonzo zoPhuhliso lwamaKhondo – uKwakhela uMzantsi Afrika iNkqubo esebenzayo neDibeneyo yeeNkonzo zoPhuhliso lwamaKhondo, kwiPhephandaba loMbuso ukwenzela ukuba uluntu luhlomle.Le drafti yoMgaqo-nkqubo xa sele iphunyeziwe iza kubonelela ngendlela edibeneyo yeenkonzo zophuhliso lwamakhondo asemgangathweni kubo bonke abemi. Oku kuza kubenza bakwazi ukuthatha izigqibo zokukhetha amakhondo afanelekileyo ukuze bakwazi ukufumana imisebenzi, benze igalelo kuluntu nasekwakheni uqoqosho lwaseMzantsi Afrika.IiNkonzo zoPhuhliso lwamaKhondo zidlala indima engundoqo ekuncedeni umsebenzi wokubonelela ngemisebenzi nenkqubo yezemfundo ukuba zifikelele kwiinjongo zazo ezijongise kuzo. Oku kwenza igalelo ekuphumezeni izindululo zesiCwangciso seSizwe soPhuhliso sokukhuthaza uhlumo lwezoqoqosho nemisebenzi, ukuthelekisa abasebenzi abaphangelayo nemisebenzi; nokwandiswa kophuhliso lwezakhono kunye noninzi lwabo banezidanga zasedyunivesiti.2.4. IKhabhinethi yazisiwe ngokuphunyezwa kweNkqubo yoVavanyo yeSizwe yaza yamkela iNgxelo yonyaka wama-2014/15 kunye nesiCwangciso soVavanyo seSizwe sesihlanu seminyaka yowama-2016/17 ukuya kowama-2018/19, njengenxalenye yendlela yokuphucula unikezela lweenkonzo.INkqubo yoVavanyo yeSizwe ivela kwiNkqubo-sikhokelo yoMgaqo-nkqubo woVavanyo weSizwe oqinisekisa ukuba ubungqina bovavanyo obuthembakeleyo nobungakhethe-cala buyafakwa kwiinkqubo zokucwangcisa, ukwenza uhlahlo lwabiwo-mali, ukuphuculwa kweziko, uhlaziyo lomgaqo-nkqubo, kwakunye nolawulo lwenkqubo oluqhubekayo nolawulo lweprojekthi ukuze kuphuculwe indlela ekusetyenzwa ngayo.  Luvavanyo olungama-47 eselugqityiwe okanye olusaqhutywayo, lubandakanya imali ezibhiliyoni ezingama-75 zeerandi enkcitho karhulumente. Zingxelo zovavanyo ezili-15 esezamkelwe ize ibe zezi-12 ezithiwe theca phambi kweKhabhinethi ukuza kutsho ngoku. Izicwangciso zokuphucula ezili-14 ziyaphunyezwa kwaye inkqubela kuzo ibonakalisa iinguqu ezinkulu, ukuvula umsantsa wempembelelo.2.5. IKhabhinethi yazisiwe ngeziphumo zovavanyo nezindululo ezamkelweyo ngenjongo yokwandisa impembelelo yokuphunyezwa kweCandelo lezeNtlalo leNkqubo yeMisebenzi yoLuntu eNatyisiweyo (i-EPWP-SS).  Le Nkqubo idlala indima ebalulekileyo kukhuseleko lwezentlalo lwabantu abadala abakwaziyo ukusebenza abangaphangeliyo, ngokubatsalela kumsebenzi onenkqubela baze bafumene ukuvuzwa kwenyanga okungumyinge we-R 1 517. 69. Uninzi lweenkqubo zecandelo lezentlalo zibonelela ngeenkonzo ezikhethekileyo kuluntu oluhlelelekileyo noluhluphekayo. I-EPWP-SS ikhule ngokuphindaphindeneyo phakathi kwesigaba sokuqala nesigaba sesibini, ukususela kwiinkqubo ezintlanu ukuya kwezi ngama-22 nezikhokelele ekubeni eli candelo lifikelele kumathuba emisebenzi engama-175 769 kwisigaba sokuqala namathuba emisebenzi engama-866 246 kwisigaba sesibini, ngaphezulu kakhulu kuma-750 000 ebesizimisele kuwo.Ezi zindululo zigxile ekuphuculeni ukuphunyezwa ukuze kunciphe ukungathathwa kakuhle kwezigqibo, ukuphunyezwa (iintlawulo nokuvuzwa) nokubonelelwa koqeqesho, kwakunye nokuququzelela.2.6. IKhabhinethi yazisiwe ngeziphumo yaza yazivuma izindululo zokuyilwa nokuphunyezwa kovavanyo lweSibonelelo soPhuhliso lezokuHlaliswa eziDolophini neSicwangciso sokuPhuculwa kwaso.Ezi ziphumo zixhasa ukugcinwa kweSibonelelo soPhuhliso lezokuHlaliswa eziDolophini njegesibonelelo soxabangelo nesiyinkxaso-mali yombono obanzi wezokuhlaliswa koluntu ozinzileyo nolinganayo ukwenzela ukuba kuxhamle abahluphekileyo namakhaya amkela imivuzo ephakathi.Oku kuyanceda ekufikeleleni kummiselo ovela lwiNkqubo-sikhokelo yesiCwangciso-qhinga sesiGaba esiphakathi; ukubonelela ngamathuba ezindlu azizigidi ezi-1 495 kwimeko yezokusingqongileyo yokuhlala okusemgangathweni ngowama-2019.2.7. IKhabhinethi iyamkele ingxelo engokuphunyezwa nokuVavanywa kweMpembelelo yezobuChwepheshe nemiCimbi yezaBasebenzi yeNkqubo yezoShishino, indlela abalawuli abayihlangabeza ngayo le ngxelo nesiCwangciso sokuphucula esifakwe liSebe lezoRhwebo noShishino.Le Nkqubo yaveliswa liSebe lezoRhwebo noShishino ngowe-1992 ukomeleza uphuhliso lwezakhono kwezenzulu-lwazi, ezobunjineli nobuchwepheshe. Yenza igalelo ekudalweni kwemisebenzi ngokuvelisa igquba labaphandi-nzulu abanezakhono ezikudidi oluphezulu nabalawuli bezobuchwepheshe boshishino nangokuphucula ukukhuphisana kwemibutho yamashishini athatha inxaxheba.Le Nkqubo iyasebenza, ibalulekile kwaye iluphawu olusebenzayo kwinkxaso yokuvelisa izinto ezintsha ebonelela ngexabiso elivakalayo ngemali ngokophuhliso lwezobuchwepheshe nophuhliso lwemicimbi yezabasebenzi nezakhono ezihambelana noshishino.
