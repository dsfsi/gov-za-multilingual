Abakhiqizi abakhasako beSewula Afrika nabathumela iwayini ngaphandle kwemikhawulo yelizwe bayazuza ngokuzibandakanya kweSewula Afrika.21.3. Emkhakheni wewayini, umnqopho kukwandisa indawo yokukhiqizwa kwewayini, ukusekela abakhiqizi abasakhasako nokuqothela amamakethe athile ngokuthumela imikhizo ngaphandle kwemikhawulo yelizwe.22.
