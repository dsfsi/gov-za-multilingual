Ukulethwa koguquko ku-AU kuyinqubo eqhubekayo, futhi ezinye zezincomo zayo seziqalisiwe, ikakhulukazi maqondana nokuzibophezela ekutheni amazwe angamalungu e-AU kube yiwo afaka uxhasomali olukhulu enhlanganweni.
