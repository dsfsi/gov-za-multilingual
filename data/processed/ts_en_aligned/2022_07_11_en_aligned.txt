Draft Legal Sector Code (Draft LSC) Cabinet approved the publication of the Draft LSC for public commen The objectives of these codes are to facilitate the transformation of the legal sector to achieve representation as per the country s demographic The code will also ensure the development of the body that will produce well-trained and competent providers of legal service The development of the Draft LSC was overseen by the Legal Practice Council, a statutory body established in terms of Section 4 of the Legal Practice Act, 2014 (Act 28 of 2014) and guided by the Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment Act (B-BBEE), 2013 (Act 46 of 2013).
The B-BBEE Act of 2013 provides for the Minister of Trade, Industry and Competition to issue codes of good practice on black economic empowerment in the specific sector Members of the legal profession currently do not have a sector code to guide them in addressing the imbalances and inequalities in the legal professio The Draft LSC will be published in the Government Gazette for 60 days to allow for public comment 2.
