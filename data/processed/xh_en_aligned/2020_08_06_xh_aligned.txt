ngeli xesha lobhubhane we-COVID-12.

Iziko lokuququzelela elikhethekileyo elisanda kusekwa kutsha nje lijolise ekomelezeni amalinge adibeneyo phakathi kwee-arhente zokunyanzeliswa komthetho ukuba zithintele, zicuphe, ziphande zitshutshise naluphi na urhwaphilizo olunxulumene ne-COVID-19.
