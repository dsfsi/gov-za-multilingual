Urhulumente ukhombe uluhlu lwezinto eziza kukhokeliswa phambili ngowama-2017/18 eziza kugxininisa ekuqinisekiseni ukuba iinkcitho kwiziseko ezingundoqo ayehli, ukuphucula amaphulo ophuhliso lwezakhono, ukugcina isixa semali echithwa ebantwini abangathathi ntweni singatshintsh Umsebenzi wokukhangela iindawo ezisebenza kakuhle neendlela zokonga kule minyaka ishiyekileyo ye-MTEF uza kuhlala uqhuba Okwesithandathu, i-Lekgotla iye yaxoxa nangomsebenzi osele wenziwe kunye namanyathelo aza kulandelwa kwiphulo lokuzisa utshintsho kwiinkampani zikarhulumente (ii-SOC) ukusukela ngeyoMdumba wowama-2015.
Oku kwenziwe ngenxa yendima ebalulekileyo edlalwa zi-SOC kumsebenzi wokutshintsha ngamandla uqoqosho nentlalo okugxininiswa kuwo ngulo mbuso wathatha intambo zolawulo ngeyeSilimela ngowama-2011.
