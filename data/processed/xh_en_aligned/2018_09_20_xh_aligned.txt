Izenzo zobundlobongela

Ubundlobongela obenzeke eendaweni ezininzi zeli abamkelekanga kwaphela kwaye bunxamnye futhi bukhabana noMgaqo-sisek IKhabhinethi iwuqhwabela izandla uMbutho wamaPolisa oMzantsi Afrika (i-SAPS) ngokuba abambe ngokukhawuleza abo kurhaneleka ukuba bathathe inxaxheba kwizenzo zobundlobongela obenziwe esidlangalaleni kunye nabo bafunyenwe beneempahla ekukrokreleka ukuba zibiw 9.
Ukuqhambuka kwe-Klebsiella
IKhabhinethi ithi akuhlanga lungehliyo kwiintsapho zeentsana ezintandathu ezithe zasweleka ngethuba kuqhambuka isifo i-Klebsiella pneumoniae eThelle Mogoerane Hospital eVoslooru Esi sifo noko ngoku siyalawulek UMphathiswa wezeMpilo, uGq Aaron Motsoaledi, sele ebhengezile ukuba ezi ntsana kunye noonina bazo ziza kutshintshwa zithunyelwe eNelson Mandela Children's Hospital naseCharlotte Maxeke Johannesburg Academic Hospita Oku kuza kunika ithuba isebe lezempilo lephondo ukuba licoce lize libulale iintsholongwane zesi sifo kwesi sibhedlela, futhi lilungise neengxaki ezinxulumene nendlela esisebenza ngayo esi sibhedlela ngaphandle kokuphazanyisw Izigqibo zeKhabhinethi10.
