IKhabhinethi ivume ukuba uMthetho-sihlomelo oYilwayo we-Postbank yoMzantsi Afrika ka-2020 upapashwe ukuze uluntu lukwazi ukunika izimvo zalo ngaw LoMthetho oYilwayo ulungisa uMthetho we-Postbank Limited yoMzantsi Afrika, ka-2010 (uMthetho 9 ka-2010) okhoyo ngok
Lo Mthetho oYilwayo wenza ukuba kwazeke ukusekwa kwe-Postbank Holding Company yoMzantsi Afrika ngokwemigaqo yoMthetho weeBhanki ka-1990 (uMthetho 94 ka-1990).
Le nkampani iza kwenza ukuba kwazeke ukubandakanya amalungu oluntu akhutshelwe ngaphandle ngenxa yezezimali akwazi ukuthabatha inxaxheba enentsingiselo kwiintshukumo zoqoqosh

USuku looMama lweHlabathi
IKhabhinethi nayo njengehlabathi iza kubhiyozela uSuku looMama lweHlabathi ngomhla we-8 kweyoKwindla 2021.
Olu suku lusetyenziselwa ukujonga emva kwinkqubela eyenziweyo ukufikelela kulingano ngokobuni kwaye lukwangqamana nesikhumbuzo sama-25 sokwamkelwa koMgaqo-siseko woMzantsi Afrika ka-1992.
Ukususela ngo-1994, sithabathe amanyathelo okuphumeza imigaqo-nkqubo neenkqubo ezijolise ekuphuculeni amalungelo abantu basetyhini kwaye sisazimisele ukuqinisekisa ukuba abantu basetyhini banikwa amandla kwezoqoqosh
