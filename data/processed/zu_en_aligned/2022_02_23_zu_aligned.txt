INkulumo Yesabelomali Sikazwelonke

Isabelomali sihlinzeke ngemininingwane ethe xaxa kanye nolwazi olubanzi mayelana nezibophezelo ezenziwa nguMongameli u-Cyril Ramaphosa eNkulumweni yakhe Echaza Isimo Sezwe (i-SoNA) yowezi 2022 ayethula ngoLwesine mhla ziyi-10 kuNhlolanja 2023.
