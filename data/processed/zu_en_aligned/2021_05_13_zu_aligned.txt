Uqinisa ukuphoqelelwa kokuhlinzekwa kwabahloli kanye nobungozi obukhona emsebenzini kubasebenzi bezindizamshin Uphinde uhlose nokuzilinganisa nemiyalo nokusebenza kahle kwe-Ejensi Yomhlaba Yezamandla Enuzi i-International Atomic Energy Agenc INingizimu Afrika iyilungu le-International Atomic Energy Agency
