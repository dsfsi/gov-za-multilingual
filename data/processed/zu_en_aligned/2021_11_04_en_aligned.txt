Criminal Law (Forensic Procedures) Amendment Bill of 2021
Cabinet approved the submission of the Criminal Law (Forensic Procedures) Amendment Bill of 2021 to Parliament for processin The proposed amendments provide for the mechanism to enforce the taking of DNA buccal samples (samples from cells on the inside of the person s cheek) from people convicted under Schedule 8 offence
Schedule 8 offences include sexual offences, robbery, human trafficking and culpable homicid The collected buccal samples will facilitate future prosecutions for reoffender
The Bill will also enable the establishment of a comprehensive forensic DNA database to strengthen the fight against crime by capacitating the police with evidence to investigate and solve serious crime
