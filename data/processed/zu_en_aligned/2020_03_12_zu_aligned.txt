Igciwane i-Corona (i-COVID-19)
IKhabhinethi liphinda liqinisekisa iNingizimu Afrika iyonke ukuthi kwenziwa konke okusemandleni ukuvikela izwe kukho konke ukuqubuka kwe-COVID-19.
UNgqongqoshe Wezempilo, uDk Zweli Mkhize, ubikele iKhabhinethi ukuthi kunezigameko eziyi-13 ze-COVID-19 eNingizimu Afrik Namuhla ekuseni, uNgqongqoshe Wezempilo uqinisekise ukuthi sekunezigameko ezintsha ezine esezihlonziwe, okwenza isibalo sezigameko ze-COVID-19 sibe yi-17.
Uzohlinzeka ngeminye imininingwane ngokuhamba kosuk IKhabinethi ligcizelela okushiwo yi-World Health Organisation noNgqongqoshe Wezempilo uMkhize, ukuthi bonke abantu baseNingizimu Afrika kumele baqhubeke nokuthatha izinyathelo zokunqanda ukudluliseka nokusabalala kwaleli gciwan Izindlela zokuzivikela zibandakanya ukuvama ukugeza izandla ngensipho namanzi noma usebenzisa uketshezi olwakhiwe ngamakhemikhali lokuhlanza izandla (alcohol-based hand sanitizer), zibandakanya ukugwema ukuthinta amehlo, ikhala nomlomo ngezandla ezingagezwanga nokumboza umlomo ngethishu uma ukhwehlela noma uthimula bese uyilahle leyo thish Izimpawu ezijwayelekile zegciwane zibandakanya, ukushisa komzimba ngokweqile, ukukhwehlela kanye nokuphefumula kanzim Uma umuntu enezimpawu ezingenhla njengoba kuchazwe ngenhla, abantu belulekwa ukuthi bahlale emakhaya futhi bafune usizo lwezempil Sinxusa bonke abantu ukuthi bahloniphe ubumfihlo beziguli ze-COVID-19 kanye nemindeni yaz Sinxusa bonke abantu ukuthi bagweme ukusabalalisa ulwazi olungamanga ngaleli gciwane ukuze kugwenywe ukwanda kokwesaba,isihlava nokucwasa emiphakathini yeth Abantu abakhathazekile bangathinta iNombolo Yocingo ye-National Institute for Communicable Diseases (i-NICD) ku-0800 029 999 ukuze bathole ulwazi oluyilona nge-COVID-14.
IKhabhinethi lihlanganyela noMongameli Cyril Ramaphosa ekufiseleni ithimba lokubuyisela abantu ezweni kanye nabasebenzi be-South African Airways (i-SAA) abasuka ngoLwesibili belibangise eDolobheni Elikhulu lase-Wuhan, esiFundazweni sase-Hubei e-People's Republic of China, ukuyolanda izakhamizi zaseNingizimu Afrika eziyi-122.
