Dintlha dingwe tsa botlhokwa e tla bo e le maemo a kagiso le tshireletso mo Aforika, go lwantsha bolwetse jwa Ebola le seabe sa loago le ikonomi mo kontinenteng, metswedi mengwe ya matlole go Kitlano ya Aforika, go amogelwa ga Dingwaga tsa Ntlha tse Lesome tsa Leano la go Tsenngwa Tirisong ga Maikaelelo a AU a 2063 le popose wa ya Dit habakopano mo Dingwageng tsa yona di le 7.
