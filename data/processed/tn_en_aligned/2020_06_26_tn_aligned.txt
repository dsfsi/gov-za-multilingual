Leano la Phaloso go Kgwebo ya Tirelophofo tsa Aforika Borwa (SAA)Kabinete e amogetse pegelo ya tswelopele ka ga tsamaiso phaloso go kgwebo ya SAA go tswa go IMC ka ga SAA, e e eteletsweng pele ke Tona ya Dikgwebo tsa Set haba, Rre Pravin GordaKabinete e dumela gore tlhopo e e namatsang go tswa go baadimisi go wetsa tsamaiso ya phaloso go kgwebo e santse e le itlhophelo e e maleba ebile e le bonako, go khamphani ya puso go fetola merero ya yona eo e tsenyeletsang kgwebo ya yona, sekoloto sa yona le maikarabelo a mangw Kabinete e dumela gore tirelophofo e e fetotsweng e tla latela lenaneo le le fetogileng jaaka tlhokagalo ya dit hono go tsweledisa bakgweetsi ba difofane ba bathobantsho morago ga dingwaga di le 26 mo temokerasing ya ron
E tshegetsa tshitsinyo ya tirelophofo e nt hwa le maiteko a a akaretsang go batlana le matlole go tswa go metswedi e e farologaneng, go tsenyeletsa le babeeletsi ba ba ka nnang gon Eno ke yona tsela e le nngwe fela eo go ka yona tirelophofo e nt hwa, e e tswelelang, e e gaisanang e e ka neelanang ka ditirelo tse di gokagantsweng tsa selegae, kgaolo le tsa bodit habat haba2.
IkonomiKabinete e amogela tekanyetsokabo e e baakantsweng ya tlaleletso ya 2020/2021 e e tlhagisitsweng ke Tona ya Matlotlo, Rre Tito Mboweni ka Laboraro, 24 Seetebosigo 2020.
Seno se bakilwe ke leroborobo la Bolwetse jwa Mogare wa Corona (COVID-19) le dipoelo tsa go wela tlase ga ikonom Tekanyetsokabo ya Tlaleletso e tlhagisa tselammapa go fokotsa sekoloto ka go tokafatsa mekgwa ya go reka le go tlhama motheo wa tsosoloso ya ikonomKabinete e na le tsholofelo ya gore maitlamo a a itsisitsweng ke Tona Mboweni a tla tsepamisa ikonomi ya rona go nna mo tseleng e e popota le go tswelela, bogolosegolo fa mongwe le mongwe a ikamaganya le puso mo go a tsenyeng titrisong Tsa se weng ka ga COVID-19 Kabinete e amogetse pegelo ya tswelopele go tswa go Lekgotla la Naga le le Jarileng Maikarabelo a Mogare wa Coron Jaaka karolo ya tsibogelo go Maemo 3 a kgonagalo e kgolo ya kotsi ya letsholo la taolo ya motsamao wa batho la boset haba, go tlhagisitswe diphetolo go melao go ya ka melawana ya Molao wa Taolo ya Matlhotlhapelo, 2002 (Molao 57 wa 2002).
Le fa Cabinete e itumelela tiro e e tswileng diatla e e tsweletseng go dirwa go tshola selekanyo magareng ga go tsweledisa matshelo le go a boloka, e atlenegisitse go tsenngwa mo kaseteng ga diphetolo tse di amanang le go bulwa ga direset hurente, dihotele le dikhasin.
Kabinete e boeletsa piletso go botlhe go dira mmogo go emisa go anama ga ditshwaetsego mo baaging ba rona ka go sala morago ditsamaiso tsa boitekanelo tse di jaaka go tlhapa diatla, sekgala go kamano ya loago le go rwala sesirasefatlhego fa ba le mo mafelong a botlhe Tirisodikgoka e e Ikaegileng ka Bong (GBV) Kabinete e tshwentswe ke go tlhagelela ga palo e e kwa godimo ya dikgetse tsa GBV go ralalal nag Go latela dipolao tsa basadi le bana tse di sa tswang go tlhagelela, e ipileditse go batho botlhe mo Aforika Borwa
