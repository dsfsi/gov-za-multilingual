Merero ya ga Jaana1.
Maemo a Boset haba a Matlhotlhapelo
Puso ya Aforika Borwa e tsibogetse leroborobo la lefatshe lotlhe la Bolwetse jwa Mogare wa Corona (COVID-19) ka go goeletsa Maemo a Boset haba a Matlhotlhapelo ka Mopitlwe 2020, go ya ka Molao wa Taolo ya Matlhotlhapelo, 2002 (Molao 57 wa 2002).
Se se dirile gore go tsewe dikgato tse di tsamaisanang le tsa dinaga tse dingwe le tse di rulagantsweng sentle go sireletsa matshelo a batho le ditsela tse ba iphedisang ka tson
Naga e ntse e le ka fa tlase ga melawana e fa e sale ka Mopitlwe 2020, fa Aforika Borwa e ne e bega kgetse ya yona ya ntlha ya mogare
Moporesitente o begile ditsereganyo tse dingwe tse maikaelelo a tsona e leng go thusa naga go tsena mo legatong le le wa la taolo ya leroborobo la COVID-19.
Dikgato tsa mo malobanyaneng tsa go atolosiwa ga nako ya go nna mo tirisong ya Maemo a Boset haba a Matlhotlhapelo go fitlha ka 15 Moranang 2022 go tsaya tsia tlhokego ya go tswelela go fetola melao e e leng teng le dithulaganyo tse di maleba tse di dirwang ke ditheo tsa puso go samagana le seabe sa matlhotlhapelo
Kabinete e ikuetse gore go tsewe tsia melawana ya boitekanelo go laola mekgwataolo ya pabalesego e e seng ya melemo jaaka go apara dimmaseke, katologano fa gare ga batho le taolo ya dikokoano gammogo le go entiw E tla re letla gape le go tlhoma mekgwathuso ya tshoganyetso e e bonako, e bile e nonofile go lwa kgatlhanong le bogale ba COVID-19.
Go Entelwa COVID-19
Kabinete e gakolotse batho botlhe mo Aforika Borwa go nna ba tsibogile ka ntlha ya fa COVID-19 e ise e fenngwe, mme e ikuetse go botlhe ba dingwaga tse 12 le go feta gore ba entiw Go entiwa e santse e le tshireletso ya rona e e gaisang kgatlhanong le mogare o, e bile go fokotsa kotsi ya bolwetse jo bo masisi, go amogelwa kwa bookelong le los
Le fa tota ditshwaetsego le dintsho di le kwa tlase ga jaana, re lebagane le kgonego ya lekhubu la botlhano mo dibekeng tse di tlan Tshireletso e re nang nayo fela ke bopaki ba saense bo bo supang maatla a meento go sireletsa matshel Go fitlha ga jaana, naga ya rona e setse e tlhabile meento ya COVID-19 e feta dimilione tse 33.
Kabinete e amogetse gape le Melawana ya Dikgato tsa go Tlhokomela go Tshwaediwa ke COVID-19 e e tshwanetseng go dirisiwa kwa ditirong mme e rebotsweng ke Lefapha la Merero ya Ditiro le Badir Melawana eno e ne ya simololwa go kwalwa morago ga ditherisano le ba Lekgotla la Boset haba la Tlhabololo ya Ikonomi le Merero ya Badiri mme e tla tsenngwa tirisong fa Maemo a Boset haba a Matlhotlhapelo a sena go emisiw
Melawana eno e tla kaela bathapi le bathapiwa fa ba kwala kgotsa ba tsenya mo dinakong leano la bona la tshekatsheko ya dikotsi go fokotsa ditshwaetso tsa COVID-19 mo ditiron E neelana ka dikgato tse di jaaka go entiwa, go katogana ga batho le go iphepafatsa go sireletsa mafelo a tir 3.
Khonferense ya Peeletso ya Aforika Borwa (SAIC)
Kabinete e amogetse balalediwa go tswa matlhakoreng otlhe a naga le go ralala lefatshe kwa SAIC ya bone e e tshwerwe ka Labone, 24 Mopitlwe 2022 kwa Johannesbur
Khonferense e e baya mo pepeneng dit hono tse dintsi tsa peeletso le tse di molemo tse Aforika Borwa e di rebolelang babeeletsi, mo pakeng e e mabapi le go godisa Tumelano ya Kgwebisano e e Senang Dikgoreletsi mo Dinageng tsa Kontinente ya Aforika (AfCFTA), e e rotloetsang kgwebisano mo teng ga Aforik
Dipeeletso go tswa ka fa nageng le tsa dinaga tsa bodit haba di na le seabe sa botlhokwa mo kgolong ya ikonomi le mo go tlholeng ditiro tse di tla nnelang rur SAIC e itsege ka go tlhola ditiragalo tsa ikonomi, ditiro le ditlhokwa tsa badirisi e leng dilo tse di tshegetsang kgolo ya ikonomi ya nag
Dikhonferense tsa rona tse di fetileng tsa peeletso di ngokile palogotlhe ya ditsholofetso tsa go beeletsa tse di ka dirang diranta di le dibilione di le 774.
Mo diporojekeng tsa peeletso di le 152 tse di begilweng, di le 45 di setse di konoseditswe mme di le 57 tsona di a tswelel
Kabinete e tshepa gore khonferense ya monongwaga e tla oketsa dipeeletso mo dipeeletsong tse re setseng re di bone jaaka e le karolo ya maitlhomo a naga a go ngoka diranta di le ditirilione di le tse di tla beelediwang mo dingwageng di le tlhano tse di tlan 4.
Ditlamorago tsa Ntwa ya Russia le Ukraine mo Ikonoming
Kabinete e sekaseka ditlamorago tsa ntwa ya ga jaana magareng ga Russia le Ukraine tse re tla nnang natso mo ikonoming ya naga, ka ntlha ya fa ditlamorago tsa yona di itemogelwa mo mebarakeng le mo ditlhwatlhweng go ralala lefatsh
Tsela e e botoka ya go sireletsa ikonomi le ditsela tsa ka fao batho ba rona ba iphedisang ka tsona ke go tswelela ka go tsenya tirisong Leano la Kagose wa le Tsosoloso ya Ikonomi (ERRP).
Puso e ikemiseditse go dirisa tsotlhe tse e nang natso go tshegetsa Maaforika Borwa kgatlhanong le ditlamorago tsa ditlhwatlhwa tse di tlhatlogang tsa botshelo le go netefatsa gore ikonomi ya rona e emelelana le dinako tse tsa ditsuatsue
Fantisi ya Makhubu a a Tlhokegang Thata a Kgaso
Kabinete e amogetse go konosediwa ka katlego ga fantisi ya ntlha ya naga ya makhubu a a tlhokegang thata a kgaso ka Bothati bo bo Ikemetseng ba Ditlhaeletsano ba Aforika Borwa (ICASA).
Madi a a kana ka diranta di le dibilione di le 1a a tswang mo fantising eno a tla tsenngwa mo matloleng a naga go tshegetsa maano a naga a a botlhokwa a a akaretsang go tsosolosa ikonomi, go tlhola ditiro, twantsho ya bonweenwee, bosenyi le ya tirisodikgoka e e tobaneng le batho ba bong jo bo farologaneng gammogo le dipolao tsa basad
Go abelana ka makhubu a mangwe a a tlhokegang thata go tla itlhaganedisa go rebolwa ga dithekenoloji tse di wa jaaka 5G, go fokotsa tlhwatlhwa ya data le go netefatsa kgolagano e e anameng ya inthanet Go rebolelwa laesense ga makhubu a ma wa go tsamaisana le maikarabelo a puso e nang le ona mo baaging a go dumalanweng ka ona a go tsenya inthanete mo dikolong tsa puso, mo maokelong le mo ditei eneng tsa sepodisi mo dingwageng tse tharo tse di tlan 6.
Letsatsi la Ditshwanelo tsa Botho
"Kabinete e lebogile Maaforika Borwa otlhe ka go tsenela mekete ya Letsatsi la Ditshwanelo tsa Botho ka Mosupologo, 21 Mopitlwe 2022 ka fa tlase ga setlhogo: ""Ngwaga wa go Tshwaragana re le set haba mmogo le go ipopa se wa: re tsholetsa le go sireletsa ditshwanelo tsa rona tsa batho""."
Go tsweletsa ditshwanelo tsa botho mo nageng ya rona ke selo se se botlhokwa thata mo go ageng set haba, e leng thulaganyo e re tshwanetseng go e somarela le go e sireletsa ka gal Ka go farologana ga rona, re ka gagamatsa go tshwaragana ga rona le go aga set haba se se tshwaraganen
Ka mowa o o tswelelang wa tirisanommogo, jaaka re ya re lebile Kgwedi ya Kgololosego ka Moranang, Kabinete e ikuela go Maaforika Borwa otlhe go nt hwafatsa ntwa kgatlhanong le bosemorafe, kgethololo ya bosemorafe, letlhoo la batswantle le go se itshokelane go go tlhagelelang mo makaleng a bona a a farologanen 7.
Go Thapiwa ga Moatlhodimogolo wa Aforika Borwa
Kabinete e amogetse le go akgola Motlatsa Moatlhodimogolo wa maloba, Raymond Zondo, ka go thapiwa ga gagwe jaaka Moatlhodimogolo wa Aforika Borwa go tloga ka 1 Moranang 2022, le go mo eleletsa masego mo maikarabelong a gagwe a ma w
Kabinete e amogetse gape le maikaelelo a ga Moporesitente Cyril Ramaphosa a go tlhopha Moatlhodi Mandisa Maya, e leng Moporesitente wa Kgotlatshekelokgolo ya Boikuelo, mo maemong a Motlatsamoatlhodimogolo fa phatlhatiro eo e buleg 8.
Katlholo ya Kganelo ya Nakwana e e Rebotsweng Kgatlhanong le Thuso ya Matlole go Rephaboleki ya Cuba
Kabinete e nopotse kganelo ya nakwana e e rebotsweng kwa Kgotlatshekelokgolo ya Pretoria, go thibela thuso ya botho e Aforika Borwa e neng e dumetse go e rebolela Rephaboliki ya Cub Morero o o tla bo o le fa pele ga dikgotlatshekelo gape mo matsatsing a le 20 mme puso e tla neela letlhakore la yona ka dingangisano tseno ka motsi o
Botsalano ba Aforika Borwa le Cuba bo tswa kgakala mo nakong ya kgaratlho ya kgololosego mo kgaolong ya ron Fa e ne e se ka go tswiwa thuso ke batho ba Cuba ka go ema nokeng dinaga tsa Borwa jwa Aforika sebaka se se fetang dingwagasome tse tharo, go ka bo go ne go tsere sebaka se se leele thata go ka golola kgaolo e mo kgatelelong ya bokolonial Moporesitente wa maloba, Nelson Mandela, o ne a tlhaloganya se sentle, ke ka moo Cuba e nnileng naga ya ntlha kwa ntle ga kontinente e, e Madiba a neng a e jela nala fa a sena go gololwa kwa kgolegelong ka 1990.
Cuba e ne ya intsha setlhabelo se se seng kana ka sepe go thusana ntwa le mekgatlo ya kgololosego mo Aforika ka nako e naga e nnye e ya setlhakatlhake e neng e na le sebaka sa dingwaga di feta sometlhano e ntse e kgaratlha ka fa tlase ga dikiletso tsa ikonomi tsa Amerik Mathata a ikonomi ya Cuba a ntse a etegela mme puso ya Cuba e tlhoka thus E setse e le dingwagasome di le dintsinyana ntse Mexico, Bolivia le Russia e le tse dingwe tsa dinaga tse di neetseng naga eno ka thuso ya botho go tlhofofatsa matlhotlhapelo a ikonomi a setlhakatlhake sen
Beke e, Kabinete e inyalanya le go gopola Ntwa ya Cuito Cuanavale kwa Angola e e lwelweng go tloga ka 1987 go fitlha ka 1988.
Cuba e nnile le seabe sa botlhokwa mo go thuseng mekgatlo ya kgololosego mo Aforika go fitlhelela maitlhomo a yon Go ne go se dipoelo dipe go batho ba Cuba ba ba neng ba tla go thusa, mme poelo ya bona e kgolo e ne e le go lwela kgololosego ya Borwa jwa Aforika go tswa mo digankeng tsa bokolonial Moporesitente wa nako eo, Fidel Castro, o ne a tlhotlhelediwa ke keletso ya phetogo mme o ne a bolelela balekanenae gore ba lwela
morero o montle go gaisa wa batho .
Go tloga kwa Angola go ya Namibia, le Algeria go ya Guinea Bissau, Cuba e nnile le seabe sa botlhokwa thata mo go tliseng kgololosego ya dinaga tse go tswa mo taolong ya bokolonial
Cuba e tsweletse go tlamela ka thuso ya boitekanelo le tse dingwe mo Aforika Borwa morago ga 1994, go sa le gale mo leroborobong la COVID-19, mme ke maikarabelo a rona a botho go nna seoposengwe le batho ba Cuba ka nako eno e matshelo a bona a leng boim 9.
Leeto ka Motlotlegi Kgosi Letsie III
Kabinete e nopotse le go amogela leeto la botsalano go tswa go Motlotlegi Kgosi Letsie III wa Naga ya Lesotho jaaka a etetse Moporesitente Ramaphosa ka Labotlhano, 11 Mopitlwe 2022 kwa Pretori
Moporesitente Ramaphosa, ka maemo a gagwe a Dinagatlhabololo tsa Borwa jwa Aforika jaaka Motsereganyi kwa Pusong ya Lesotho, o ne a buisana le Motlotlegi Kgosi Letsie III ka dikamano tsa dinaga ka bobedi le tswelopele tebang le tsamaiso ya tsereganyo ya SADC mo Nageng ya Lesoth Go solofelwa gore Basotho, ka tsereganyo ya ga Moporesitente Ramaphosa, ba tla dira ka natla go konosetsa tirego ya diphetogo pele go tshwarwa ditlhophokakaretso tse di rulaganyeditsweng go tshwarwa ka Lwetse 2022.
Ditshwetso tsa Kabinete1.
Tumelano ya Maphatamantsi go Tsenya Tirisong Mekgwataolo e e Amanang le Tumelano ya Lekgetho go Thibela Tshenyo ya Motheo le Phaposo ya Merokotso (BEPS MLI)
Kabinete e amogetse go romelwa ga BEPS MLI kwa Palamenteng go mametlelelw Jaaka nngwe ya dinaga tse 95 tse di nang le seabe mo tumelanong e e tlhomilweng ke Mokgatlo wa Tirisano le Tlhabololo ya Ikonomi (OECD) go fokotsa dit hono tsa go tila lekgetho le tshenyo ya motheo ka ditlamo tsa dinagadintsi, Aforika Borwa e saenile tumelano ya lekgetho ka Seetebosigo 2017.
Fa tumelano e ya lekgetho e sena go mametlelelwa, Aforika Borwa e tla e dirisa mmogo le ditumelano tse dingwe tsa lekgetho tse e di saenileng le dinaga tse dingw
Aforika Borwa ke tokololo ya OECD mme e ikemiseditse go dirisana le dinaga tse dingwe go thibela merokotso ya ditlamo go ntshiwa mo teng ga naga ka bonweenwee go e isa kwa tikologong ya lekgetho le le kwa tlase kgotsa e e senang lekgetho gotlhelel 2.
Go Kwalolwa Se wa ga Tumelano ya Dithulaganyo tsa Lekgetho Magareng ga Naga ya Aforika Borwa le ya Kuwait
Kabinete e amogetse go romelwa kwa Palamenteng ga Tumelano e e Kwalolotsweng Se wa ya Dithulaganyo tsa Lekgetho Magareng ga Naga ya Aforika Borwa le ya Kuwai Diphetogo mo tumelanong e, e e tseneng tirisong ka 2006, di dirilwe le go saeniwa ka 2021.
Tumelano eno ya lekgetho e samagana le go tila go duelela lekgetho gabedi le thibelo ya go tila melao ya matlole mo mererong ya makgetho a dinaga tse ka bobed
Ntle le go samagana le dikgato tsa go tila go duelela lekgetho, ditumelano tseno gape di rotloetsa gore dinaga ka bobedi di se nne le mahunelo fa go tla mo mererong ya go duelela lekgeth 3.
Togamaano e e Golaganeng ya Thibelo ya Bosenyi le Tirisodikgoka (ICVPS)
Kabinete e amogetse go tsenngwa tirisong ga ICVP Togamaano e e tla dira jaaka sediriswa sa Kaedi ya Puso ka ga Pabalelo le Tshireletso e e amogetsweng ka 2016.
E atlenegisa go nna le seabe ga puso le set haba go lwantsha bosenyi le go thibela tirisodikgok
ICVPS e na le dipilara tse thataro tse di tshegetsanang e bile di nyalana, mme tsona ke (1) bosiamisi bo bo nonofileng ba bosenyi; (2) ditsereganyo tsa go sa le gale go thibela bosenyi; (3) ditsereganyo tse di tshegetsang batswasetlhabelo; (4) thebolelo ya ditirelo e e nonofileng e bile e golagane; (5) pabalesego ka tolamiso ya tikologo le (6) go nna le seabe ga baagi le set haba go go bonalan Togamaano e e dumela gore go na le tlhokego ya ditsereganyo tsa tlaleletso go samagana le ditlhotlheletsi tsa bosenyi jaaka botlhoki, go se lekalekane, botlhokatiro, katlaatleloloago le thut
E a akaretsa fa go lejwa bobedi mafelo a metsesetoropo le a metsemagae, go akaretsa makgotla a setso, jaaka bannaleseab E setse e rerisanetswe le dikarolo tsotlhe tsa puso, kgwebo, ditheo tsa ditlhotlhomiso le mekgatlo ya baag Togamaano e e amogetsweng e, e tsena boemong ba Togamaano ya Boset haba ya Thibelo ya Bosenyi ya ga jaan 4.
