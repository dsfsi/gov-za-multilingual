Accession to the 2003 United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) Convention for the Safeguarding of Intangible Cultural Heritage (ICH)
Cabinet approved South Africa s accession to the 2003 UNESCO Convention for the Safeguarding of IC
The convention provides for the safeguarding, preservation and promotion of ICH by the respective member state
The ICH includes amongst others community cultural spaces, knowledge, objects and artefacts in communitie
The convention obliges member states to identify, define and devise appropriate measures for their preservatio It also encourages countries to adopt legal, technical administrative and financial measures to safeguard and promote IC The accession will be tabled to Parliament for ratification
