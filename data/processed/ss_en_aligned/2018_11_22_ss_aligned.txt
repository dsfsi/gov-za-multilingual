Libhuloho iMaputo-Catembe
IKhabhinethi ibongela bonjiniyela baseNingizimu Afrika lebebakhona ngesikhatsi kwakhiwa libhuloho lelingumlandzo lelidze kunawo onkhe e-Afrik IMaputo-Catembe Bridge yakhiwe be-China Road and Bridge Corporation kantsi ihlanganisa iMaputo eMozambique neKwaZulu-Nata
Mengameli waseMozambique, Mnu Filipe Nyusi, uvule lelibhuloho lelingemkhilomitha lamatsatfu budze iMaputo-Catembe Bridge, lelakhiwa iminyaka lendlula kulemine ngetigidzi letilinganiselwa ku-US$783.
