Sigaba se-I (2017) kuGazethi yaHulumende kute kutsi ummango utfole litfuba lekuphawula ngalLenchubomgomo iphendvula kusimemo seLuhlelo Lwekutfutfukisa Lwavelonkhe (i-NDP) sekugcizelela kakhulu ekucanjweni kwalokusha, kukhicita lokwentiwe kwaba ncono, kufuna ngemandla mnotfo losekelwe kulwati, nekusetjentiswa kancono kwematfuba ekucatsanisa nekucudzelanLeNchubomgomo ye-IP yinchubomgomo lebalulekile ekukhutsateni kucamba lokusha, kwendlulisa bucwepheshe, lucwalingo nekutfutfukisa, imisebenti yekucamba, kuvikela batsengi, kutfutfukiswa kwetetimboni kanye nekukhula kwemnotfo Ikhabhinethi ivumile kutsi kushicilelwe Luhlaka Lwelisu Letitfutsi Letinganukubeti Imvelo kute kutsi ummango uphawule ngal Lelisu leli litawakha sisekelo sekwentiwa kwenchubomgomo yangekhatsi kumkhakha wetitfutsi mayelana nekunciphisa kutfucutiswa kwe-GHG (umoya lonukubeta sibhakabhaka).
Inhloso yalelisu kunciphisa lifutse letitfutsi kumvelo kube futsi kulungiswa tidzingo tetitfutsi tamanje netesikhatsi lesitako lokumiselwe kumitsetfomgomo yekutfutfuka lokusimemTinchubo tetitfutsi tingumgogodla wemisebenti yaseNingizimu Afrika yetemnotfo netenhlalo ngekwenta kutsi bantfu nemikhicito bakhone kuhamb Nanoma kunjalo, imimoya lengcolile lekhicitwa ngumkhakha wetitfutsi yenta-1 wemoya longcolile lotfucutwako (i-GHG), bese kutsi titfutsi temgwaco tona titfunyisa i-GHG lenge-9.
INingizimu Afrika yetsembise kunciphisa kutfucutiswa kwemoya longcolile we-GHG nge-34% nga-2020 ngange-42% nga-2026.
