Diphetho t e bohlokwa t a Kabinete
Kabinete e dumelet e go phatlalat wa ga Leano leo le Thadilwego la go Rarolla T hilafat o ya Moya ka Dit habeng t e di Tlet ego ka Batho t e di Hwet ago Moputso wa Fase ka Afrika Borwa gore set haba se fe ditshwaotshwao ka ga son Leano le le nyaka go fa mokgwa wo o nepi it ego wa maikemi et o wa go rarolla maemo a godimo a t hilafat o ya moya ka dit habeng t e dingwe t eo di tlet ego ka batho t e di hwet ago moputso wa fas
Le fa mokgwa wo o kopant wego wa go tsenya tiri ong masolo a go fokot a go be a dikgong ka malapen Tsenogare ye ke go netefat a gore mafapha a a obamela Melawana ya Boset haba ya Boleng bja Moya Tikologon Leano le le nepi it wego le tla thekga leano la bjale la boset haba go rarolla t hilafat o ya moya ka mo nagen
Kabinete e dumelet e go swara mmogo ga Khonferentshe ya bo 21 ya Bodit habat haba ka ga AIDS (AIDS 2016) magareng ga mmu o le Mokgatlo wa Bodit habat haba wa AIDS ka International Convention Centre (ICC) ka Durban go tloga ka la 18 go fihla ka la 22 Mosegamanye 2016.
Go swarwa ga khonferentshe ye go tla t wet a pele phetolo ya naga ye mabapi le HIV le bolwet i bja mafahla mola ka go le lengwe go lebelet we kudu ye nngwe ya ditlhohlo t e bohlokwa kudu ka Afrika Borwa, ka seleteng le lefasen
Khonferentshe ye e tla fa sefala seo go sona banyaki i i go ralala lefase ka bophara ba tlago abelana dit welopele t a mahlale t a moragorago ka mo lefapheng le, go ithuta ka ga maitemogelo a bona le go hlama maano a go t wet a pele mekgwa ka moka ya masolo a rena ka moka a go alafa le go thibela HI 3.
