Uyilo loMthetho-siHlomelo oYilwayo weQumrhu leSizwe eliLawula ezeNyukliya wowama-2023.

Lo Mthetho uYilwayo wenzelwe ukuba uhambelane noMthetho we-NNR wowe-1999 uze ulandele imigaqo esetyenziswayo ye-Internation Atomic Energy Agency (i-IAEA).
